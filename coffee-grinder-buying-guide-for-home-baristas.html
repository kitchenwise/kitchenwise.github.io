Burr vs Blade Grinders

Blade grinders chop beans unevenly, creating inconsistent extraction. Burr grinders crush beans between two surfaces, producing uniform grounds that lead to better taste — especially for espresso and pour‑over coffee.

Choosing by Brew Method

  • Espresso: Fine grind with micro adjustments
  • Pour‑over: Medium to medium‑fine burr grinder
  • French Press: Coarse grind consistency

Final Verdict

If you’re serious about coffee, a burr grinder like the Baratza Encore is the best long‑term investment for flavor and consistency.

On a tight budget, a blade grinder works—but upgrading later will bring a noticeable improvement.
